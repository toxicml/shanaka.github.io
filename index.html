Intelligent Road-Sign Line-Following Robot

PID line following, road-sign AI, obstacle handling

A compact robot that follows a black line with an 8-sensor PID controller, reads road signs, and makes junction decisions based on ordered sign positions. A mobile app selects destination and modes (manual, line follow, obstacle handling), while the robot adapts speed to limit signs.

Vision runs two parallel paths: OCR on red circular ROIs for speed signs (20, 100), and a lightweight Random-Forest classifier (HOG+HSV) for symbolic signs (BUS STOP, P, TRAIN). Results are fused to trigger stops, pauses, and turn choices at the 4-way junction.

MEMS Single-Cell Injection Platform

Monolithic injector + microgripper with force feedback

A compact MEMS platform that integrates an electrostatic comb-drive injector, a dual-arm microgripper, and embedded piezoresistive force sensing for precise, force-regulated single-cell manipulation. Stroke exceeds 5 µm with biologically safe injecting forces and high dynamic stability.
